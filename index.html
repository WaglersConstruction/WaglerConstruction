Metal Roofing

Durable, low-maintenance roofing with clean workmanship and attention to details that prevent leaks.

What we typically handle

  • Roof replacement (removal of old roofing as needed)
  • New installs on additions, garages, and outbuildings
  • Trim, ridge cap, and flashing details
  • Leak troubleshooting and limited repairs (case-by-case)

Every job is different. We will recommend a practical solution based on roof condition, pitch, and ventilation.

What we ask for (to quote quickly)

  • Address or town (service area)
  • Approximate roof size (or building dimensions)
